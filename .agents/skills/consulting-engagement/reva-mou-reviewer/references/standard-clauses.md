# REVA University — Standard MOU Clause Library

Ready-to-insert preferred language for common MOU provisions. Use these as Tier 1 redlines when the contract deviates from REVA's standard position.

---

## 1. Parties and Authority

**REVA standard opening:**
> *"This Memorandum of Understanding ("MOU") is entered into between REVA University, a University established under the REVA University Act, 2012 and recognised by the University Grants Commission under Section 2(f) of the UGC Act, 1956, having its principal offices at Rukmini Knowledge Park, Kattigenahalli, Yelahanka, Bengaluru – 560 064, Karnataka, India, represented by its Vice Chancellor / Registrar [Name], duly authorised in this behalf ("REVA University" or "Party A"), and [Full legal name of counterparty], [a university established/company incorporated] under the laws of [jurisdiction], having its principal offices at [address], represented by [Name and Designation] ("Partner" or "Party B").*

> *Each party represents and warrants that it has full authority to enter into this MOU and that the signatory executing this MOU on its behalf is duly authorised to do so."*

---

## 2. Non-Exclusivity Savings Clause

> *"Nothing in this MOU shall be construed to create an exclusive relationship between the parties or to limit either party's right to enter into similar memoranda, agreements, or collaborative arrangements with any other institution, university, organisation, or individual."*

---

## 3. Intellectual Property

**Pre-existing IP:**
> *"Each party shall retain full ownership of all intellectual property rights in materials, inventions, software, data, know-how, and other work product developed independently of this MOU ("Background IP"). No transfer of ownership in Background IP is intended or shall be inferred from this MOU. Each party grants the other a non-exclusive, non-transferable, royalty-free licence to use its Background IP solely to the extent necessary to carry out the activities contemplated by this MOU."*

**Jointly developed IP:**
> *"Intellectual property jointly developed by the parties in the course of activities under this MOU ("Jointly Developed IP") shall be jointly owned by the parties in equal shares, unless otherwise agreed in writing in a specific project agreement. Neither party shall license, assign, or otherwise transfer its share of Jointly Developed IP to any third party without the prior written consent of the other party."*

**Student IP:**
> *"For the avoidance of doubt, intellectual property created by students of REVA University in the course of their academic studies, including dissertations, thesis work, and student projects, shall vest in the respective student in accordance with REVA University's academic regulations and applicable UGC guidelines, and shall not be subject to any claim by the Partner."*

**Publication rights:**
> *"Each party's researchers and faculty shall retain the right to publish the results of collaborative research in academic journals, conference proceedings, and other scholarly outlets. Where a party reasonably requires a review period to identify patentable inventions or to protect confidential information before publication, the other party shall provide written notice of such requirement, and publication may be delayed for a period not exceeding [90/180] days from the date of such notice. After expiry of this period, the publishing party may proceed with publication."*

---

## 4. Confidentiality

**Mutual confidentiality with academic carveout:**
> *"Each party ("Receiving Party") agrees to maintain in confidence all non-public information disclosed by the other party ("Disclosing Party") in connection with this MOU that is designated as confidential or that a reasonable person would consider confidential given the nature of the information and circumstances of disclosure ("Confidential Information"), using no less than the same degree of care it uses to protect its own confidential information, and in no event less than reasonable care.*

> *Confidential Information shall not include information that: (a) is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Receiving Party; (b) was known to the Receiving Party prior to disclosure; (c) is independently developed by the Receiving Party without use of or reference to the Confidential Information; or (d) is received from a third party without restriction.*

> *Notwithstanding the foregoing, each party may disclose Confidential Information: (i) to the extent required by applicable law, court order, or government authority; (ii) as required for compliance with the requirements of NAAC, NBA, AICTE, UGC, or any other accreditation or regulatory body having jurisdiction over REVA University; (iii) under the Right to Information Act, 2005, to the extent REVA University is subject to its provisions.*

> *The obligations of this clause shall survive termination of this MOU for a period of [three (3)] years."*

---

## 5. Term and Termination

**Standard term clause:**
> *"This MOU shall come into effect on the date of the last signature below and shall remain in force for a period of [three (3) / five (5)] years ("Initial Term"), unless earlier terminated in accordance with this clause.*

> *This MOU may be renewed for successive periods of [one (1) year] by mutual written agreement of the parties, executed no later than [90] days before the expiry of the then-current term.*

> *Either party may terminate this MOU for convenience by providing [90] days' written notice to the other party, without penalty or liability to the terminating party beyond obligations accrued as of the notice date.*

> *Either party may terminate this MOU for cause upon [30] days' written notice if the other party materially breaches this MOU and fails to cure such breach within [30] days of receiving written notice specifying the breach in reasonable detail.*

> *Upon termination or expiry: (a) all ongoing student and faculty exchange activities shall be allowed to continue until their scheduled completion or for a period not exceeding [6] months, whichever is earlier; (b) each party shall return or destroy the other party's Confidential Information; (c) clauses relating to intellectual property, confidentiality, governing law, and dispute resolution shall survive."*

---

## 6. Data Protection

**DPDPA 2023-compliant clause:**
> *"To the extent that either party processes personal data of the other party's students, faculty, staff, or other individuals ("Personal Data") in connection with this MOU, each party shall: (a) process Personal Data only for the purposes set out in this MOU and in accordance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 ("DPDPA") and other applicable data protection laws; (b) implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect Personal Data against unauthorised access, loss, or destruction; (c) not transfer Personal Data to any third party without the prior written consent of the disclosing party; (d) notify the disclosing party without undue delay upon becoming aware of any breach of security affecting Personal Data; and (e) delete or return all Personal Data upon termination of this MOU, unless retention is required by applicable law.*

> *For cross-border transfers of Personal Data (if applicable): Personal Data shall be transferred to countries permitted under the DPDPA 2023 or pursuant to adequate safeguards as may be prescribed."*

---

## 7. Governing Law and Dispute Resolution

**Standard REVA clause:**
> *"This MOU shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India. The courts at Bengaluru, Karnataka shall have exclusive jurisdiction over any dispute that is not resolved through the dispute resolution procedure set out below.*

> *In the event of any dispute, controversy, or claim arising out of or relating to this MOU, or the breach, termination, or validity thereof, the parties shall first attempt to resolve the dispute through good-faith negotiation between senior representatives of each party, within [30] days of written notice of the dispute.*

> *If the dispute is not resolved through negotiation, the parties shall submit the dispute to mediation in accordance with the Mediation Act, 2023, with a mediator appointed by mutual consent, for a period not exceeding [30] days.*

> *If mediation fails to resolve the dispute, it shall be finally settled by arbitration under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996 (as amended), with a sole arbitrator appointed by mutual agreement or, failing agreement within [15] days, by [MCIA/DIAC/BCCI]. The seat of arbitration shall be Bengaluru, Karnataka. The language of proceedings shall be English. The arbitral award shall be final and binding on the parties."*

## 8. Anti-Bribery and Compliance

> *"Each party represents, warrants, and covenants that: (a) it shall comply with all applicable laws, regulations, and codes of conduct in the performance of its obligations under this MOU; (b) neither it nor any of its directors, officers, employees, or agents has made, offered, or agreed to make any payment, gift, or benefit to any government official, political party, or any other person in connection with this MOU that would constitute a violation of the Prevention of Corruption Act, 1988 (India), or any other applicable anti-corruption or anti-bribery legislation; and (c) it shall promptly notify the other party if it becomes aware of any actual or suspected breach of this clause."*

---

## 9. Force Majeure

> *"Neither party shall be liable for any failure or delay in performing its obligations under this MOU to the extent such failure or delay is caused by circumstances beyond that party's reasonable control, including natural disasters, pandemics, acts of government, civil unrest, or war ("Force Majeure Event"), provided that: (a) the affected party provides written notice to the other party within [14] days of the onset of the Force Majeure Event; (b) the affected party uses reasonable efforts to mitigate the impact of the Force Majeure Event; and (c) if a Force Majeure Event continues for more than [90] days, either party may terminate this MOU by [30] days' written notice without liability, subject to the wind-down provisions of the termination clause."*

---

## 10. Entire Agreement and Amendment

> *"This MOU, together with any Annexures attached hereto, constitutes the entire understanding between the parties with respect to the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, or agreements. This MOU may be amended only by a written instrument signed by authorised representatives of both parties. No Annual Action Plan or other operational document issued pursuant to this MOU shall have the effect of amending this MOU."*
