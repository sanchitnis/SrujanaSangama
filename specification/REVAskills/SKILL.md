
# SrujanaSangama

## Purpose
Institutional system to convert external opportunities into repeatable annual activities with automated routing and evidence generation.

## Skills

1. ANNUAL_ACTIVITIES_OPPORTUNITY_EXTRACTOR  
   Identifies opportunities from inbound emails and documents.

2. OPPORTUNITY_TO_ANNUAL_CALENDAR_MAPPER  
   Maps opportunities to annual institutional activity buckets.

3. AUTO_ROUTING_AND_VISIBILITY_SKILL  
   Routes opportunities to appropriate offices and stakeholders.

4. NIRF_NAAC_EVIDENCE_GENERATOR  
   Generates audit-ready evidence from participation data.

## System Flow
Inbound → Extract → Map → Route → Evidence

## Governance
System Owner: VC / Registrar / IQAC  
Review Cycle: Annual
