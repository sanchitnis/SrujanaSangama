# REVA University Brand Guidelines

Reference this skill whenever creating any content — HTML, presentations, documents, social posts, or visual artifacts — for REVA University.

---

## Brand Colours

| Name        | Hex       | RGB             | Pantone       | CMYK                  |
|-------------|-----------|-----------------|---------------|-----------------------|
| REVA Orange | `#f7a35b` | 247, 163, 91    | P 20-6 C      | C0 M42 Y75 K0         |
| REVA Grey   | `#4a4c55` | 58, 60, 68 (approx) | P 173-14C | C62 M53 Y42 K43   |

Always use REVA Orange as the primary accent colour. REVA Grey is used for dark backgrounds and secondary text. White (`#ffffff`) is used for reversed/light-on-dark contexts.

## Logo Usage

### Versions

| Version              | Description          | Use Case             | Source                       |
|----------------------|----------------------|----------------------|------------------------------|
| **Primary Logo** | REVA wordmark + Srivatsa symbol on white/light background | use by default | https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/5f/REVA_University_Bangalore.png |
| **Reverse Logo** | Full white logo on REVA Orange (`#f7a35b`) background | use when background is dark or clashes with primary colours | https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/5f/REVA_University_Bangalore.png |

### Rules
- Only two colour variations are permitted: Primary or full-white Reverse.
- Do **not** use the logo in any other colour, on mismatched coloured backgrounds (pink, grey, dark), or with altered fonts/styles.
- Do **not** distort, rotate, add effects, or apply opacity to the logo.
- Do **not** use the logo in brown/dark-orange, red, or dark grey background boxes.

### Clear Space
- Minimum **2x** clear space above and below the logo unit.
- Minimum **4x** clear space on the left and right sides.
- Maintain **3.5x** spacing between the Srivatsa symbol (Logo Mark) and the wordmark (Logo Type) in horizontal format.

### NAAC A+ Logo
- The NAAC A+ logo follows the same two-variant rule (Primary or full-white Reverse).
- The NAAC logo must be sized so it fits **within the height of the Srivatsa symbol** — never larger than the REVA logo.
- Align the NAAC logo top and bottom exactly with the Srivatsa symbol.

|-------------------|--------------------|-------|
| Feed Post (image) | 1080 × 1350 px     | 4:5   |
| Reels / Stories   | 1920 × 1080 px     | 9:16  |

### Logo Placement on Creatives
- **Top Left**: NAAC A+ logo
- **Top Right**: REVA University logo

### Content Rules
- Always use REVA Orange (`#f7a35b`) and REVA Grey (`#4a4c55`) as accent colours.
- Use Plus Jakarta Sans and Glacial Indifference fonts only.
- Do **not** place hyperlinks directly on the creative — use QR codes or keep links in the bio.
- Always tag REVA's official handle for collaborations; collaboration approvals are at the REVA Branding Team's discretion.

### Standard Hashtags (include on every post)
```
#REVAuniversity #EducateToEnterprise
```

## Brand Voice & Content Strategy ("Educate to Enterprise")

All content should reflect REVA's mission of instilling an enterprising mindset. Prioritise content that showcases:

- **Innovation & Research**: Student projects, patents, prototypes, hackathons.
- **Entrepreneurship**: REVA NEST startups, venture launches, pitch competitions.
- **Industry Readiness**: Placements, internships, industry workshops, masterclasses.
- **Interdisciplinary Learning**: Cross-school collaborations (Design + Tech + Management).
- **Leadership & Initiative**: Clubs, fests, national competitions, peer-learning.
- **Community Impact**: Volunteering, sustainability, civic tech, empathy-driven work.
- **Student Spotlights**: "I did this at REVA" stories, alumni journeys, bold thinkers.

Content tone: curious, confident, entrepreneurial, and practical — not promotional or corporate-generic.
