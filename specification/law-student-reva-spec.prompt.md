# Specification: `law-student-reva` Plugin

## 1. Goal Description
Detailed specification for the `law-student-reva` plugin under the SrujanaSangama private marketplace, providing academic guidelines and research coaching customized for REVA School of Legal Studies.

## 2. Scope Boundaries
- **In scope**: Legal research methodology, case briefs analysis, and moot court presentation scaffolding.
- **Out of scope**: Non-academic formal litigation drafting templates.

## 3. Verification Criteria
1. Manifest files (`plugin.json`, `package.json`) resolve correctly inside the marketplace environments.
