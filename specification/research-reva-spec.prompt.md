# Specification: `research-reva` Plugin

## 1. Goal Description
Detailed specification for the `research-reva` (SrujanaShodha) plugin under the SrujanaSangama private marketplace, representing the faculty research advisor for funding, research scoping, and citation mapping.

## 2. Scope Boundaries
- **In scope**: Scoping research proposals, DST/DBT active call mapping, and citation network diagrams.
- **Out of scope**: Direct submission to peer-reviewed journals.

## 3. Verification Criteria
1. Manifest files (`plugin.json`, `package.json`) resolve correctly inside the marketplace environments.
