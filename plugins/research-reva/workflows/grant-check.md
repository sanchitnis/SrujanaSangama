# /grant

Analyze the research draft or topic against the active funding brief (DST, DBT, UGC) for:
1. Alignment: Evaluate the proposal against the funding agency's core thematic priorities.
2. Structure: Audit the proposal sections (objectives, methodology, budget rationale) to ensure compliance with the target grant template.
3. Novelty: Verify that the draft has clear, explicit expressions of novelty, feasibility, and impact.
