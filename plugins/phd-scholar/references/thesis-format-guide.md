# REVA Thesis Format Guide
## Derived from Appendix 4, REVA PhD Regulations 2025

Source: `references/reva-PhD-regulations.md` — Appendix 4. All rules are directly derived from that document. In the event of any discrepancy, the source document takes precedence.

Referenced by: `agents/scholar/thesis-writer.md`, `workflows/06_thesis-sprint.md`

---

## Section 1 — Paper & Print

- Paper size: **A4 white**
- Print: **one-sided only**
- Print quality: **letter quality** (laser or equivalent) — no dot-matrix
- Background: **no grey, dark, or tinted backgrounds** on any page — must be plain white

---

## Section 2 — Typeface

- Font size: **minimum 11pt**
- Font style: **no script or ornamental fonts** — use standard serif or sans-serif (e.g., Times New Roman, Arial, Calibri)
- Accent marks, mathematical annotations, special characters: in **black ink only**
- Consistency: the same font family must be used throughout the body text

---

## Section 3 — Margins

| Edge | Margin |
|---|---|
| Binding edge (left) | **1.5 inches** |
| All other edges (top, bottom, right) | **1 inch** |

- Headers, footers, and page numbers may sit within the margin area, provided they are within 0.5 inches of the edge
- Margin rules apply to every page including blank pages

---

## Section 4 — Spacing

| Text Type | Spacing |
|---|---|
| Body text | **1.5× or double** (either is accepted) |
| Footnotes | **Single** |
| Tables | **Single** |
| Quotations (block-quoted) | **Single** |

---

## Section 5 — Orientation

- Primary text orientation: **portrait**
- Landscape figures, charts, tables: must be positioned so they **face away from the binding edge** (i.e., the reader tilts the thesis clockwise to read them, not anti-clockwise)
- Landscape pages must follow the same margin rules as portrait pages (1.5 inch binding edge)

---

## Section 6 — Chapter Order

The thesis must follow this chapter sequence exactly:

1. **Title page** — thesis title, scholar name, registration number, supervisor name, school, university, year
2. **Certificate** — signed by: scholar, guide, co-guide (if any), Director of Research / Principal
3. **Revised Certificate** — include only if the thesis was resubmitted after corrections
4. **Preface / Acknowledgements**
5. **Table of Contents**
6. **List of Tables**
7. **List of Illustrations** (figures, charts, maps)
8. **Abstract** — concise summary; typically 300–500 words
9. **Chapters** (numbered in Arabic)
   - Chapter 1: Introduction
   - Chapter 2: Literature Review / Related Work
   - … (as per research structure)
   - Final Chapter: Conclusions and Future Work
10. **References**
11. **Appendices** (if any)
12. **Bibliography** (if separate from References)

---

## Section 7 — Pagination

| Section | Numeral Style | Position |
|---|---|---|
| Preliminary pages (Title page → Abstract) | **Roman numerals** (i, ii, iii, iv…) | Top-right corner, ≥0.5 inch from edge |
| Body text (Chapter 1 onward) | **Arabic numerals** (1, 2, 3…) — continuous | Top-right corner, ≥0.5 inch from edge |

- **Every page must have a page number**, including blank pages, full-page figures, and appendix pages
- The title page is counted but the page number is not printed on it (starts at page i but print begins from page ii)
- Arabic numbering restarts at 1 at Chapter 1

---

## Section 8 — Submission Package

| Item | Quantity |
|---|---|
| Bound hard copies of the thesis | **5 copies** |
| Electronic version | **1 CD as PDF** |

- Binding: hardbound (standard library binding); colour of cover as specified by REVA (confirm with Research Cell)
- PDF must be text-searchable (not scanned image)
- The CD must be enclosed in a sleeve attached to the inside back cover of each bound copy

---

## Section 9 — Evidence at the End of the Thesis

The following evidence documents must be physically bound at the end of the thesis (after the Bibliography/Appendices):

| Evidence Item | Required Quantity |
|---|---|
| Journal reprint **or** journal acceptance letter | 1 |
| Conference presentation certificates | 2 |

- Evidence must be the originals or clearly reproduced copies
- Acceptance letters must be on journal letterhead (or official email printout)
- Conference certificates must name the paper title and the scholar

---

## Section 10 — Restrictions (Important — Appendix 4)

> These restrictions are mandatory and will be checked during format review.

1. **Address on the title page and in the thesis must be the school/university affiliation only.** Do not include personal residential addresses or other institutional affiliations or qualifications.

2. **The REVA University logo or symbol must NOT appear anywhere in the thesis.** This applies to the cover, title page, headers, footers, and all internal pages.

3. **No dedication page.** A dedication is not permitted in the REVA thesis format. Acknowledgements are permitted.

---

## Format Compliance Checklist

Before submitting to the colloquium or for final submission, run through:

- [ ] A4 white paper, one-sided, letter quality print
- [ ] Minimum 11pt font; no script/ornamental fonts
- [ ] 1.5-inch binding margin; 1-inch other margins
- [ ] 1.5× or double line spacing throughout body
- [ ] Landscape figures face away from binding edge
- [ ] Chapter order matches Appendix 4 sequence exactly
- [ ] Roman pagination for preliminary pages; Arabic from Chapter 1
- [ ] Every page numbered; no unnumbered pages
- [ ] 5 bound copies + 1 CD (PDF, text-searchable)
- [ ] Evidence bound at end: 1 journal + 2 conference certificates
- [ ] No personal addresses; university affiliation only
- [ ] No REVA logo anywhere in the thesis
- [ ] No dedication page
