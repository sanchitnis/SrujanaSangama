# CSE Publication Venues Guide
## REVA PhD Scholars — School of CSE / CSA

> **Preamble:** In Computer Science, top-tier conference proceedings carry research weight equivalent to high-impact journals — cite: Meho (2019), doi:10.1016/j.joi.2019.02.006. Scopus and WoS serve as baseline tracking standards in Indian academia — cite: Pranckutė (2021), doi:10.3390/publications9010012. Cross-reference all venues against the live Scopus Source List and UGC-CARE Group II portal before submission — venues shift quartiles and can be discontinued.
>
> Referenced by: `agents/scholar/publication-coach.md`, `workflows/05_publication-pipeline.md`

---

> ⚠️ **Verify before submission**
> 
> Check every venue against:
> - [Scopus Source List](https://www.scopus.com/sources.uri) — for Scopus indexing status
> - [Clarivate WoS Master Journal List](https://mjl.clarivate.com/) — for WoS/SCIE status
> - UGC-CARE Group II portal (see `references/ugc-care-guidance.md`) — for UGC approval status
>
> Venues shift quartiles and can be discontinued. A venue that was indexed at the time a paper was submitted may not remain indexed at the time the thesis is evaluated.

## Venue Index Tags

Tags used throughout this guide:
- `[Scopus]` — Indexed in Elsevier Scopus Source List
- `[WoS-SCIE]` — Indexed in Web of Science Science Citation Index Expanded
- `[WoS-ESCI]` — Indexed in Web of Science Emerging Sources Citation Index
- `[UGC-CARE]` — Listed in UGC-CARE Group II (or predecessor list)
- `[IEEE-Sponsored]` — Technically sponsored / organised by IEEE
- `[ACM-Sponsored]` — Technically sponsored / organised by ACM
