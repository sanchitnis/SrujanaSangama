# Session Type: Daily Standup — Micro-Planning & Focus Setting

**Purpose:** Quick daily or weekly planning session — yesterday / today / blockers — with 2–3 specific doable tasks as output. Anti-procrastination framing built in.

**Estimated total time:** 5–15 minutes

---

## Phase 1 — Yesterday Check (2 min)

Ask: *"Since our last session, what did you manage to do on your PhD? Even small things count."*

Listen. If nothing was done: do not add guilt. Ask: *"What got in the way?"* → route to Phase 3 (Blocker).

If something was done: acknowledge it. Update `memory/tasks.md` → mark completed items done.

---

## Phase 2 — Energy & Focus (1 min)

Ask: *"On a scale of 1–5, how is your energy today? 1 = exhausted, 5 = fully energised."*

| Score | Plan Adjustment |
|---|---|
| 4–5 | Deep work: 2–3 hour focused block |
| 2–3 | Lighter session: 1 hour, one clear deliverable |
| 1 | Route to `wellness-companion.md` before any planning |

---

## Phase 3 — Today's Focus (5 min)

Read:
- `memory/tasks.md` → current open tasks
- `context/research-tracker.md` → next milestone + days remaining

If a milestone is within 14 days: treat it as today's hard constraint.

Propose a session goal. Frame it as specific and completable:
- ✗ *"Work on the thesis"*
- ✓ *"Draft the opening 300 words of the methodology section"*
- ✓ *"Read and annotate 2 papers from the shortlist"*
- ✓ *"Run experiment set B and record results in the log"*

Ask: *"Does this feel right for today, or is there something more pressing?"*

---

## Phase 4 — Blockers (2 min)

Ask: *"Is there anything blocking you from doing this today?"*

If yes: quickly classify the blocker type (technical / methodological / administrative / resource / personal). For anything beyond a quick fix: *"Let's tackle that blocker next — shall we use the stuck-triage workflow?"* → route to `13_stuck-triage.md`.

---

## Phase 5 — Output (1 min)

Write today's mini-plan to `memory/tasks.md`:

```
## Daily Plan — [Date]
Energy: [N/5]
Milestone on horizon: [milestone + N days]

Today's tasks:
1. [specific task] — est. [N hours]
2. [specific task] — est. [N hours] (if energy permits)

Blockers:
- [list or "none"]

Check-in at end of session: [yes / no]
```

---

## Output Template

```
## Daily Standup — [Name] — [Date]

**Yesterday:** [done / not done — what happened]
**Energy:** [N/5]
**Next milestone:** [milestone — N days]

**Today's 1–2 tasks:**
1. [specific task]
2. [specific task if energy permits]

**Blockers:** [list or "none" — blocker triage if needed]

---
*Daily plan written to: `memory/tasks.md`*
```
