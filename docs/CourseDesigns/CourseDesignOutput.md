## 5. PERFORMANCE RUBRICS

### 5.1 Generic Academic Rubric

| Criteria | Excellent (4) | Good (3) | Satisfactory (2) | Poor (1) |
|----------|--------------|----------|-----------------|---------|
| **Content Accuracy** | All concepts 100% accurate; deep understanding | Most (85-95%) accurate; good understanding | Some (70-85%) accurate; gaps present | Many (<70%) inaccurate; significant gaps |
| **Completeness** | All requirements met with exceptional additions | All core requirements thoroughly met | Most (80%+) requirements met | Many (<80%) requirements missing |
| **Critical Analysis** | Excellent synthesis; original insights | Good analysis; connects ideas appropriately | Basic analysis; limited synthesis | Minimal or surface-level analysis |
| **Communication** | Exceptionally clear, logical, professional | Clear and well-organized | Somewhat clear; organizational issues | Unclear; poorly organized |
| **Real-World Application** | Outstanding real-world relevance | Good connection to practice | Attempted application; limited relevance | No apparent real-world connection |

### 5.2 Assignment/Problem-Solving Rubric

| Criteria | Excellent (4) | Good (3) | Satisfactory (2) | Poor (1) |
|----------|--------------|----------|-----------------|---------|
| **Problem Understanding** | Clear identification of all components | Identifies most components | Identifies some components; gaps | Misses major components |
| **Solution Approach** | Appropriate, efficient algorithm/method | Appropriate approach; minor inefficiencies | Partially appropriate approach | Inappropriate or flawed approach |
| **Technical Correctness** | All calculations/operations correct | Minor errors (<5%) | Multiple errors (5-15%) | Major errors (>15%) |
| **Explanation** | Clear, detailed explanation of reasoning | Explanation provided; mostly clear | Explanation present; unclear sections | Little/no explanation |

### 5.3 Project Rubric

| Criteria | Excellent (4) | Good (3) | Satisfactory (2) | Poor (1) |
|----------|--------------|----------|-----------------|---------|
| **Scope & Complexity** | Highly ambitious; exceeds expectations | Appropriately scoped; challenging | Adequate scope; moderate challenge | Limited scope; minimal challenge |
| **Technical Quality** | Excellent; production-ready | Good quality; minor issues | Acceptable quality; some issues | Poor quality; significant issues |
| **Documentation** | Comprehensive, clear, professional | Adequate documentation; clear | Basic documentation; some gaps | Poor/missing documentation |
| **Testing & Validation** | Thorough testing; edge cases covered | Adequate testing; major cases covered | Minimal testing; some gaps | Little/no testing |
| **Presentation** | Professional, compelling, well-delivered | Clear, well-organized, engaging | Acceptable; some clarity issues | Poorly presented; unclear |
