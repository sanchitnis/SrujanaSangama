## Quick Match Guide

| Faculty Type | Start Here |
|-------------|-----------|
| Early career (≤5yr), any domain | SERB-SRG or VGST-GRD |
| Early career, Karnataka | VGST-GRD (first!) → SERB-SRG |
| Women scientist | DST-WISE-KIRAN or DBT-BioCARe |
| Social science / management | ICSSR-MRP → UGC-MRP |
| Technical education domain | AICTE-RPS |
| Biotech / health | DBT or ICMR |
| India-US collaboration interest | SPARC (needs strong US partner) |
| Any domain, Karnataka | VGST first — lower competition |
| Startup-linked research | BIRAC-BIG or BIRAC-SPARSH |
