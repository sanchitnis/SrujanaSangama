## What was adapted

The structural pattern for modular, reusable skill/agent files — including the YAML frontmatter schema (`name`, `description`, `tags`, `version`, `created`), the concept of skill-based agents that can be routed to by an orchestrator, and the separation of concerns between rules, skills, and workflows — is inspired by the google-deepmind/science-skills repository architecture.

All domain-specific content (REVA University research advisory context, Indian funding landscape, SDG mapping, faculty competency profiling, personal brand building) is original work created for REVA University's SrujanaSangama AI platform.
