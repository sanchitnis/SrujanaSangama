## Predatory Journal Warning List

**Always check these before recommending any journal:**
- Beall's List: scholarlyoa.com (maintained by community)
- COPE member list: publicationethics.org/members
- DOAJ verified: doaj.org

**Red flags:**
- Unsolicited email invitation to submit
- "Rapid peer review" (24–48 hours)
- Editorial board members unverifiable or from unrelated fields
- No ISSN or fake ISSN
- APC requested upfront before review

**Journals to avoid specifically** (commonly circulated in Indian academia):
- Any journal with "World Academy of Science, Engineering and Technology" (WASET) — predatory
- "International Journal of Recent Technology and Engineering" — Scopus status disputed
- Always verify active Scopus indexing at scopus.com/sources
