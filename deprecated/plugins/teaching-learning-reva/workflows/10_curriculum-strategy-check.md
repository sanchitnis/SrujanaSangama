# /curriculum-strategy-check
## Curriculum Strategy Review & Audit

> Paste this command: `/curriculum-strategy-check <path-to-strategy-document>`
>
> **Purpose**: Rigorously audit a school's curriculum strategy document (vision, mission, PEOs, POs, job roles, market analysis) against REVA design standards and international best practices before architecture design begins.
>
> **Session Type**: Structured audit review | **Duration**: 30–45 minutes
>
> **Human Role**: You provide the strategy document. AI audits and flags gaps. Faculty deliberates improvement requests.

---

## Input Requirements

Provide a **School Curriculum Strategy Document** containing:

1. **School Vision & Mission**
   - Institutional alignment to REVA's global, sustainable, technologically advanced, wellbeing vision
   - School-specific differentiation and values
   - Student formation goals (what kind of graduate?)

2. **Programme Educational Objectives (PEOs)**
   - 3–5 PEOs per programme
   - AI-era ambition statement (how does AI co-working raise expected graduate performance?)
   - Differentiators from peer institutions

3. **Job Role Analysis**
   - 6–10 critical job roles for graduates (entry, mid-career, aspiration)
   - Skills, tools, and responsibilities per role
   - Emerging vs. declining roles (5-year horizon)
   - Salary/career progression expectations

4. **Market & Futures Intelligence**
   - WEF Future of Jobs relevance
   - NASSCOM FutureSkills gaps and opportunities
   - LinkedIn Economic Graph trends in your domain
   - QS/THE/NIRF global benchmarking analysis
   - Peer institution positioning

## Phase Gate Approval Decision

**PASS - Strategy Approved**
→ Proceed to Phase 2 (Curriculum Architecture); Faculty Committee has approved strategy and PEO framework

**CONDITIONAL PASS - Revisions Required**
→ Faculty Committee must address flagged "CONDITIONAL" dimensions before proceeding
→ Target revision date: [2 weeks]
→ Re-audit after revision

**FAIL - Major Revision Required**
→ Strategy requires substantial rework before architecture design can begin
→ Recommend: Faculty workshop to deepen market grounding and PEO AI-era framing
→ Timeframe: 4–6 weeks
→ Re-submit for audit

---

## Using This Audit

1. **Faculty Curriculum Committee Meeting**: Present audit report and discuss findings
2. **Identify Ownership**: Assign faculty members to address each CONDITIONAL or FAIL dimension
3. **Timeline**: Set 2-week or 4-week revision target depending on severity
4. **Revise**: Faculty updates strategy document with specific changes to address gaps
5. **Re-audit**: Submit revised strategy for follow-up audit
6. **Gate Approval**: When all dimensions achieve PASS or CONDITIONAL, proceed to Phase 2
